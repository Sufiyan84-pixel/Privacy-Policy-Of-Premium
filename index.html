1. Information We Collect

Premium All-in-One operates with a minimal-data philosophy while delivering powerful features. Depending on your usage, we may collect:

Account Data

Name, email, profile (when you sign up for cloud sync or premium subscription).

Usage Analytics

Anonymous feature interactions, crash reports, performance metrics to improve app stability.

Device Info

Device model, OS version, unique identifiers & app version for troubleshooting.

Local Files

Files you choose to process (e.g., PDF tools, media converter) stay on-device unless you enable cloud backup.

No sensitive data (financial or biometric) is ever collected without explicit consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to provide, improve, and personalize your Premium All-in-One experience. This includes:

  • Delivering core features: document editing, media conversion, QR scanner, and storage tools.
  • Processing in-app purchases & subscription management via secure payment partners.
  • Sending important service notifications, updates, and security alerts (opt-out available).
  • Enhancing AI-based recommendations and smart tool suggestions (anonymized).

3. Data Security & Storage

Premium All-in-One uses industry-standard encryption (AES-256 for stored data, TLS 1.3 for transmission). Your local content is never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose cloud sync. We employ:

  • End-to-end encryption for premium cloud backups (optional feature).
  • Regular security audits and penetration tests.
  • Strict access controls and anonymization of analytics.
Your privacy is non-negotiable. We never sell your personal data to third parties or use it for advertising without consent. All data processing complies with GDPR, CCPA, and international standards.

4. Third-Party Services & Integrations

Our app may integrate trusted partners to extend functionality (e.g., cloud storage, translation APIs). These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Payment processors – handle billing data only.
  • Crashlytics & analytics – anonymized app performance data.
  • Maps & location tools – only if you grant permission for location-based utilities.

We ensure all third parties provide at least equivalent data protection levels.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your region, you have the right to:

  • Access & request a copy of your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account & associated data.
  • Data portability (receive structured data).
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection & marketing emails.

To exercise your rights, please contact us through the support section inside the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Premium All-in-One is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in certain regions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located worldwide, but we ensure appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses) and data protection measures compliant with your local laws.
